# Airbnb Pricing & Revenue Analysis (Tableau)

## Problem  
Airbnb hosts need to understand how pricing, location, and property size impact revenue. However, raw listing data makes it difficult to identify where pricing is optimized, where demand exists, and how revenue trends over time.

This project answers key business questions:
- How does price vary by number of bedrooms?  
- Which locations (zip codes) drive higher pricing?  
- How does revenue change over time?  
- Is there a gap between supply and high-value listings?  

---

## Solution  
Built an interactive Tableau dashboard to analyze pricing patterns, listing distribution, and revenue trends across Airbnb listings.

The dashboard enables:
- Comparison of average price across bedroom categories  
- Visibility into listing supply by property size  
- Identification of high-value geographic areas  
- Tracking of revenue trends over time  

---

## Key Insights  
- Larger properties (5–6 bedrooms) command significantly higher prices but have limited supply  
- Revenue shows clear growth followed by stabilization, indicating demand trends over time  
- Pricing is highly concentrated in specific zip codes, highlighting premium areas  
- Most listings are in lower bedroom categories, where competition is highest  

---

## Tools  
- Tableau  

---

## Outcome  
This project demonstrates the ability to transform raw data into clear, actionable insights through effective data visualization and storytelling, supporting better pricing and market understanding.
